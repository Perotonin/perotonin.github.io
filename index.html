About DF40-U

DF40-U is a nonverbal test of general intellectual abilities. The test measures the potential for abstract thinking, including the ability to identify logical patterns, similarities, and differences. It assesses adaptability, learning capacity, and problem-solving skills, which are critical for educational and professional success.

The test consists of 40 tasks (with a parallel form, DF40-U Parallel, and a shorter version, DF20, with 20 tasks). It is suitable for a wide range of participants, including students (from upper primary school to university level) and adults of all ages. DF40-U is widely used for candidate selection in employment processes across various industries such as banking, IT, sales, and HR.

Time to complete: 40 minutes for DF40-U, 20 minutes for DF20. Scoring is objective, with each correct answer worth 1 point and incorrect answers worth 0 points.

Key Features

  • Nonverbal test suitable for diverse groups
  • Measures abstract thinking and problem-solving
  • Available in two versions: DF40-U (40 tasks) and DF20 (20 tasks)
  • Validated for use in educational and professional settings
  • Objective scoring with reliable and valid results
